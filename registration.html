The steps you have to follow in re-submitting the final version of your paper:

  • Please strictly follow IEEE Template. Use the A4 format.

The listed comments should be addressed carefully in your revision. It is mandatory for all authors to incorporate all the changes suggested by reviewers else their paper will be excluded from conference proceedings.
