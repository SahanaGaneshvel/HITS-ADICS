About ADICS

Overview

ADICS - 2026 is an IET sponsored international conference conceived to attract researchers, scientists and technologists from some of the top companies, universities, research groups, and government agencies from India and around the world to communicate their research results, inventions and innovative applications in the areas of Smart Innovation, Advance Computing, and Intelligent Computational Strategies for Data Science, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

Objectives

To explore and discuss emerging trends and technologies in intelligent computing systems, including their challenges, opportunities, and societal impact. To provide a platform for presenting original research and development work in areas such as data science, intelligent systems, and computational intelligence. To Build Technical Networks and to Encourage the Industry-Academia Interaction by engaging in knowledge exchange across disciplines.

Scope

The scope of the Conference includes all the areas of Smart Innovation, Advance Computing, and Intelligent Computational Strategies for Data Science, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Besides this, participants will also be enlightened about vast avenues, current and emerging technological developments in the field of Advanced Informatics in this era and its applications will be thoroughly explored and discussed.
